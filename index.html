DocDouble is a Chicago-based DJ and producer — a surgeon by training, a technician by nature. His sets move through deep house, tech house, stutter grooves, UK garage, speed garage, and drum & bass with clinical precision and raw floor energy.

Trained hands. Steady tempo. A sound built from the same focus that runs an OR — but unleashed after hours, when Chicago needs a pulse.

Open for bookings of all kinds. Chicago-based. Willing to travel.
