Profile

Rooted in Chicago. Built for the floor.

DocDouble is a Chicago DJ and producer — a surgeon by training, a technician by nature. The handle is half joke, half truth: steady hands, sharp timing, zero wasted motion.

His sets move through deep house, tech house, stutter grooves, UK garage, speed garage, and drum & bass — with the same focus he brings to an operating room, only unleashed after hours, when the city needs a pulse.

Open for bookings of all kinds. Chicago-based. Willing to travel.
